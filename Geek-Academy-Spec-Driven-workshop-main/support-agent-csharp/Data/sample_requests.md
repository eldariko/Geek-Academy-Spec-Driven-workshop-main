# Sample Customer Support Requests

Example incoming customer messages for testing your support agent. Paste one into
the running console app to see how the agent responds. Feel free to write your own
scenarios — these are a starting point, not a fixed test set.

---

## 1. First-month refund — enough info

From: alice@example.com
Subject: Refund for new Premium plan

Hi,

I signed up for Premium on March 30 and I'm writing on April 10. I tried it for
a few days, but it is not really what I need and I have barely used the account.
Could you refund the $49.99 charge?

Thanks,
Alice
